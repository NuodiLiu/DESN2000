Problem statement:

Create a mobile platform which allows university students and shared accommodation residents to seamlessly manage household responsibilities, expenses and housemate communication, while also enabling property managers to streamline tenancy administration and tenant communication, to reduce conflict between tenants, streamline property management, and improve the overall shared living experience.

Requirements to check against:

User-focused (from research):

Transparent chore tracking and rotation system

Structured expense splitting with specific categories

Verified housemate profiles to build trust

Safe in-app communication before sharing personal details

Compatibility-based housemate matching

Simple onboarding that all housemates can adopt easily

Multilingual support for international students unfamiliar with shared living norms

Anonymous issue raising for conflict resolution

Property listing search and filtering by location and affordability

Rent reminder and payment tracking for property managers

Broadcast announcements from property managers to tenants

Technical:

Mobile-first design

Push notifications for chore and payment reminders

Secure user authentication and identity verification

Single unified platform (not fragmented across multiple tools)

Role-based access (resident vs property manager views)

Self-determined (time/resource restrictions):

High-fidelity prototype only, no back-end required

Completed within 10-week timeline

Focused on 2-3 core features to avoid overcommitting

Legal/Ethical:

User data privacy compliance

Accessibility requirements considered in design

Secure handling of identity verification documents
