# Prototype Test Plan

Five participants will each complete all five moderated tasks at the 390 by 844 pixel mobile viewport while thinking aloud. The facilitator will observe without giving guidance. Seeded data and a demonstration code remove the need for personal information.

| Test | Task | Stories | Acceptance criteria | Responsible |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| T1 | Narrow properties with combined search criteria | US-13 | AC-US13-01 to 04 and 06 | Pranav Shanker |
| T2 | Recognise verified housemate profiles | US-11 | AC-US11-01 simulated and AC-US11-02 implemented | Chien-Kai Wang |
| T3 | Build a profile and use recalculated compatibility | US-12 | AC-US12-01, 02 and 04 to 06 | Nuodi Liu |
| T4 | Complete phone verification and message safely | US-09 | AC-US09-01 simulated and AC-US09-04 implemented | Qian Cao |
| T5 | Propose, review and activate a house rule | US-02 and US-10 | AC-US02-01 to 03, AC-US10-01, 02 and 04 | Nuodi Liu |

Each participant completes T1 to T5 in order. The facilitator records completion, time, errors and interpretation. Failed criteria are recorded by identifier and repeated after the affected screen is revised.
