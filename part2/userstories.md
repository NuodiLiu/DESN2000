## Student and Resident User Stories

**US-01. As a** domestic student managing shared living for the first time, **I want to** set up a rotating chore roster and track completion history, **so that** household responsibilities are distributed fairly and I can refer back to who has and hasn't been contributing.

**US-02. As an** international student living in a shared apartment for the first time, **I want to** see clear written rules in the app about things like cleaning the kitchen, taking out rubbish, and keeping noise down, **so that** I know exactly what I'm expected to do without needing to ask my housemates.

**US-03. As a** domestic student living in shared accommodation for the first time, **I want to** propose a structured chore system through the app, **so that** I can establish clear and fair expectations with my housemates without needing a confrontational discussion.

**US-04. As a** remote worker living in shared accommodation, **I want to** log shared expenses like groceries, electricity bills, and cleaning supplies with details of who paid, **so that** I can keep track of household spending and split costs fairly between housemates.

**US-05. As an** international student living in shared accommodation, **I want to** track outstanding balances with my housemates, **so that** I know exactly who owes me money and how much without having to ask.

**US-06. As an** international student sharing a house in Australia, **I want to** split utility bills such as electricity, water, and internet with my housemates, **so that** I can ensure I only pay my fair share and avoid confusion with local billing systems.

**US-07. As an** avid traveller currently living in shared accommodation, **I want** a household noticeboard to post updates and suggestions, **so that** I can communicate with housemates without it getting lost in a busy group chat while I'm on the move.

**US-08. As a** domestic student living away from home for the first time, **I want** a way to raise household issues anonymously through the app, **so that** I can address uncomfortable problems like bathroom usage or noise without damaging relationships with the people I live with.

**US-09. As an** international student new to Australia and unfamiliar with who to trust, **I want to** communicate with potential housemates securely through the app before sharing my personal contact details, **so that** I can evaluate compatibility and build trust without exposing myself to scams or harassment during early interactions.

**US-10. As a** musical artist living in shared accommodation who regularly plays music at high volumes, **I want to** set and view agreed house rules within the app, **so that** boundaries around noise and shared spaces are clearly established upfront and I can refer back to them to resolve disputes without confrontation.

**US-11. As an** international student new to Australia with limited local knowledge, **I want to** view verified housemate profiles with clear identity confirmation, **so that** I can feel confident I am connecting with a real and trustworthy person before agreeing to share a living space with them.

**US-12. As a** social worker with a demanding and irregular work schedule seeking shared accommodation, **I want to** filter potential housemates by lifestyle preferences such as cleanliness standards, sleep schedule, and daily routine, **so that** I can find someone whose habits are compatible with mine before committing to living together.

**US-13. As a** domestic student moving out of home for the first time, **I want to** search and filter property listings by location and affordability, **so that** I can find suitable and trustworthy housing near my university without being overwhelmed or misled by inaccurate listings.

**US-14. As a** young professional living in shared accommodation, **I want to** automatically receive in-app reminders when I have an overdue chore or expense, **so that** I can stay on top of my responsibilities without housemates needing to confront me directly.

**US-15. As a** tradesperson frequently relocating for work and seeking reliable shared accommodation, **I want to** read honest reviews left by previous housemates, **so that** I can make a well-informed decision about who I choose to live with and avoid potential conflicts before committing to a shared living arrangement.

## Property Application and Management User Stories

**US-16. As a** property manager, **I want to** send automated rent reminders and monitor whether each tenant has paid their rent on time, **so that** I can reduce late payments without needing to manually chase tenants.

**US-17. As a** property manager overseeing multiple student rental properties in Sydney, **I want to** publish detailed and accurate rental listings on the platform, **so that** students searching for accommodation can easily find, evaluate, and enquire about my properties directly without the need for third party listing websites.

**US-18. As a** property manager overseeing multiple student rental properties, **I want to** send announcements and maintenance updates directly to all tenants in a property through the app, **so that** I can efficiently communicate important information without the delays and inefficiencies of individual emails or phone calls.
